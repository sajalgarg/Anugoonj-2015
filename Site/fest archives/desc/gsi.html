GROUP SONG(INDIAN)


This event will throw light on various forms of Indian music which will touch your soul.

Get ready for some sizzling combination of Taals and Surs as the music groups of different colleges of IPU take you on a ride to soothe your senses.

So get ready to be mesmerised by some awesome performances!!
RULES

1.Minimum number of participants in a team is 6, and maximum 10.

2.Maximum of 3 accompanists allowed.

3.Time Limit: 8 + 2 minutes (including stage set up)

4.Judgment will be based on quality of singing, coordination and not on makeup, costume or actions of the team.

5.No film songs allowed.
