ON THE SPOT PAINTING


A beautiful way of expressing your feelings and thoughts is through colours.

So put some elegant colours on your dreams and imagination on the canvas to bring out some masterpiece.

So get ready to paint the world as it's your chance to turn plain paper to beauty!!

1. 2 participants will be selected per zone.

4.Emphasis on creativity and relevance to the topic
