CLASSICAL VOCAL SOLO (Hindustani & Carnatic)


This is one of the most competitive events of the ANUGOONJ.

No matter how much the western music may progress, the purity of classical music always remains in our hearts.

This event requires proper command over the musical pitch, notes and the synchronisation with the rythm of the song.

Budding musicians from all colleges will try to fight for the pride.

So be ready for this beautiful traditional music event at ANUGOONJ'15.
RULES

1.Classical Hindustani and Carnatic only.

2.Number of participants is 1.

3.Maximum number of accompanists is 2.

4.Sufficient thought and care must be exercised in the choice of raga and composition.

5.Judgment will be based on the qualities like swara, taal, selection of raga, composition .
and general impression.
6.Max. Time Limit : 5+ 2 minutes
