CHOREOGRAPHY


Dance is a form of language and the person who best understands it is a choeographer.

This event will test the imagination skills of the best dancers of IPU!!

So brace yourselves to experience some finest and innovative moves from the students of different colleges of IPU!!
RULES

1.Minimum of 8 participants, maximum 14 (including 2 accompanists)

2.Time Limit: 7 + 5 minutes (including stage set up time).

3.Extra 1 minute will be given for explaining the theme.

4.No lyrics allowed, only instrument. Some words may be allowed.

5.Props allowed.

6.Folk dances not allowed.

7.The theme should have an original expression through music and movement
